However, some features are enabled and may not be needed. For example, Session and ViewState are not really needed for simple pages (i.e. if your ASP.NET page is basically hosting a single page application in Silverlight). To do this, just open the .aspx (markup) file and add the last two attributes:

<%@ Page Language="c#" AutoEventWireup="true" CodeBehind="Foo.aspx.cs" Inherits="Acme.Foobar" EnableSessionState="false" EnableViewState="false" %>
